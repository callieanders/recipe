Easy Pumpkin Pie Dip

This Pumpkin Pie Dip is a crowd pleaser during the fall months, and a big hit with anyone who is obessed with everything pumpkin. The best part: it's only three ingredients and less than 10 minutes before it is ready to eat. Serve with apples, animal crackers, cookies, pretzels or your creative snacking preference.

  • Time:
    >10 min
  • Tools:
    Bowl
    Rubber Spatula
  • Diet:
    Gluten Free
  • Serving:
    Til your heart's content
  • Ingredients
  • 1 package/8 oz Cool Whip (thawed)
  • 1 can/15 oz Pumpkin
  • 1-2 tsp (to your preference) Pumpkin Pie Spice
  • 1 small package Jello brand vanilla instant pudding
  • Step 1
    Empty can of pumpkin, instant pudding mix (dry-do not make the pudding), cool whip and spice in a large mixing bowl.
  • Step 2
    Gently fold all ingredients until combined (about 5 minutes) careful not to overfold.
  • Step 3
    If you're not ready to enjoy right away, chill in refridgerator in cool whip or other airtight container.
